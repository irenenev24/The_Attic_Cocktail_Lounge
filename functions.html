Functions @ The Attic

Thanks to the addition of our new function room, we now cater for small and large groups.

We can offer private parties for up to 90 people in our function room, seat groups as large as 50 in our restaurant or,
if your planning a large event, we can hire out the entire restaurant and inter-connecting function room which can seat 140 people.

We also offer personalised menus on request.

A list of allergens is available at all times behind the bar.
