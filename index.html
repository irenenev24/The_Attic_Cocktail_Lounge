The Attic:

Cocktail Bar & Restaurant

First established in 1948, The Attic began its life as a small bar above a greengrocer's in Manchester, England. Hidden down a side street in the city centre, The Attic was frequented by local tradesmen and politicians alike.
Preferred by many for its moody interior and intimate corners, it was a place to get business done, make new acquaintances and socialise in private.

Fast forward to modern times and The Attic is a favourite on the Manchester social scene.
A vibrant, bustling restaurant featuring seasonal fare and paired with indulgent cocktails.
